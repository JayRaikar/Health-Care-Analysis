Demographic KPIs

Health Indicators
1.Average BMI and BMI Category Distribution (e.g., Underweight, Normal, Overweight, Obese).
2.Chronic Disease Prevalence (% of people with chronic diseases).
3.Average Sleep Duration across different age groups or genders.

1.Smoking Rate (% smokers vs. non-smokers).
2.Exercise Frequency Analysis (% who exercise daily, weekly, never, etc.).
3.Diet Quality Breakdown (% Poor, Good, Excellent).
4.Alcohol Consumption Patterns (% None, Low, Moderate, High).
